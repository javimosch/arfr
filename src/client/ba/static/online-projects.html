Diagnostical.fr

Diagnostical is a home inspection booking platform, already implemented in many departments (50 independent certified inspectors in metropolitan France). Founded by an inspector and a web entrepreneur, this startup tends to literally revolutionize the profession of home inspector by a very simple process : digitization and uberization. If you are a home inspector: Offer your expertise on Diagnostical, and you will gain new customers quickly, regularly and free of charge. If you are the owner: You can access the certified inspector schedules available in your department and at the right price
