MisitioBA is created in 2015 after investing five years in the software industry working inside IT outsourcing companies in Argentina.

Cronology

To provide web development to private investors and young startups and to create real value for people.

A world with decentralised power, with a lot of small enterprises working on green projects and doing networking between. A collaborative enviroment where freelancers were the foundations.
